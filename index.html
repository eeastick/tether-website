What Tether does

For owners

Heart disease means frequent check-ins, anxious nights, and the constant question of whether your dog is okay right now. Tether monitors continuously, so you're not flying blind between appointments.

For vets

Clinical decisions deserve clinical data. Tether gives veterinarians continuous insight into cardiac and respiratory function, without hospitalizing the patient to get it.
